☄️ All About Asteroids! ✨

Asteroids are space rocks! They are leftovers from when our solar system was just getting started, about 4.6 billion years ago.

Most of them live in the "Asteroid Belt," a cosmic racetrack between the planets Mars and Jupiter. 🪐

They come in all shapes and sizes. Some are tiny like a pebble, while the biggest one, Ceres, is so huge it's also called a "dwarf planet"! 🌍

Scientists study asteroids because they can teach us about the history of our amazing universe!
